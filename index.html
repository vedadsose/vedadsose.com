About me

I’m a software engineer at heart who loves products and loves to build. Right now my main focus is serving as CTO and Co-Founder of OriginalVoices, where I’m leading the charge in scaling real-time audience intelligence powered by Digital Twins.

I believe in a builder’s mindset. Leaders should stay close to the product and the code. For me it’s about building strong teams, designing scalable architecture, and fostering a culture where ideas can quickly become reality.
